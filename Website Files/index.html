Welcome!
If you have ever taken a foreign language course, or gone to a website in a foreign language, you have probably tried google-translating foreign text into English. And you have probably been confused by the result; we certainly have. In our project, we attempt to discover what in foreign languages google finds so difficult to translate, and in which languages it finds more success.
The Harry Potter Series
J.K. Rowling is a British novelist and her 7 part Harry Potter series is the best selling book series of all time. The books have won numerous awards and honors and have sold over 400 million copies worldwide. They have been translated into dozens of languages.
